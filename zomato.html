Menu Browse (-28%)

28% of users exit before exploring the menu. Root cause: irrelevant restaurant cards on the home feed. Fix: personalized ML-ranked restaurant suggestions.

Add to Cart 54%
▼ 18% drop — 1.8M sessions lost

Add to Cart (-18%)

Users browse but don't add items. Key friction: menu lacks context (no photos 32% of items, no portion size). Fix: enriched menu data + "order again" shortcuts.

Checkout 41%
▼ 13% drop — 1.3M sessions lost

Checkout (-13%)

Cart abandonment spike at address & delivery estimate screens. Fix: pre-fill address from last order, show real-time ETA before checkout entry.

Order Placed 38%
▼ 3% drop — payment failures & second thoughts

Order Placed (-3%)

Final drop at payment. Most failures: UPI timeout (42%), card decline (31%). Fix: smart payment fallback + one-tap UPI retry without re-entering checkout.
