Growth AARRR Strategy

X (Twitter) Growth Strategy

Analysis & Product Strategy

AARRR Funnel Mapping

I mapped out the end-to-end flow from discovery to referral. The platform has enormous attention, but significant friction points exist across Activation and Retention.

Detailed Problem Statement

The Core Challenge: X's growth opportunity is not about attracting more attention—it already dominates public discourse. The real problem is an inability to convert massive public attention into trusted, personalized, and long-term engagement.

Acquisition leakage: External traffic via viral screenshots is not converting to signups.

Activation friction: Immediate feed confusion and information overload delays the user's "aha moment," causing early churn.

Activation & Retention Blockers

During Activation, new users face severe information overload and low recommendation transparency. In Retention, both toxicity and an algorithm trust deficit cause creators and premium users to churn.

Strategic Epics to Drive Growth

To convert massive public attention into trusted engagement, I formulated three core product initiatives mapped to the most critical AARRR bottleneck stages.

RICE Prioritization

Using the RICE framework, I scored and ranked the epics. "Smart Onboarding & Personalized Feed" ranked #1 with a score of 129.6 because it touches the widest cohort and directly solves the largest early-retention leakage.

The Strategic Recommendation

X's growth opportunity is not about attracting more attention—it already dominates. The real challenge is converting that attention into trusted, personalized engagement.
